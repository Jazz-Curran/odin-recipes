Burgers

Description

That first bite of a burger can change your life. Burgers are so classic, so obiqitious that you forget about how good they can be.

I am going to talk about my perfect burger. People can have varying opinions on what they like, and that is okay. But for me the sharp bite of a pickle is necessary for an awesome burger

Ingredients

  • 2 angus beef burger patties
  • 1 crispy seseme seed bun
  • Fresh lettuce
  • A ripe tomato
  • Two long slices of pickle
  • Two rashers of bacon
  • More cheese than you are expecting
  • A deep fried hashbrown
  • A sprinkling of crispy onions

Steps

  1. Start by frying the bacon on a low heat
  2. Add the burgers to the same pan to soak up the jucies
  3. Once medium rare, toast the buns
  4. Add a layer of lettuce and a layer of tomatoes
  5. Add the first burger, then a layer of bacon and cheese
  6. Add the second burger and the pickles
  7. Add the sprinkling of crispy onions, and the top bun
  8. Enjoy!
